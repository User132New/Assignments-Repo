Dream Vacation.

My dream vacation is to visit a place with a nice icefall. I imagine the beauty of ice falling , creating a magical atmosphere full of diamond-like small ice-crystals.

The attraction of such a place will be breathtaking to experience. Also the thrilling activities that can be performed. Playing with ice, building a Snow-Man and what not... Being surrounded by cushy piles of ice, jumping in them, fills me with excitement.

Not only it would allow me to connect with the environment, but it will give me a unique and different way to mother nature.

At night, I see myself right by a fireplace, sipping hot coffee, and looking at a starry sky . The thought of this dream vacation fills me with happiness and dreams.
