Delicious Recipe

Refreshing recipe for lemon juice:
  • 2 fresh lemons
  • 4 teaspoon sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2.5 cups of cold water
  • Ice cubes (if it's summer)
  • salt as per taste

Instructions:

  1. Cut the lemons in half, remove any seeds and juice them using a juicer or by hands.
  2. In a jug, combine the fresh lemon juice, sugar, and cold water. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  3. Taste the lemon juice and adjust the sweetness to your preference by adding more sugar if needed.
  4. Add salt as you prefer.
  5. Add ice cubes to the juice for a colder drink.
  6. Voila!!, Pour the lemon juice into glasses, and enjoy!!!!
