My Favorite Book: "Hear Yourself" by Prem Rawat.

Why my favourite.?
"Hear Yourself" by Prem Rawat is a book, that have made a big impact in my life. This book shares the beauty of experiencing life in the present moment. Prem Rawat, a renowned speaker and author on how to connect with one's inner self and find true peace within.

My favourite message/Quote:
It is very difficult to stop the noise between our ears, we are busy worrying about what other people think, about yesterday and tomorrow when life exists in today. Learn from children how to live in the present. Children know how to live life in every moment.


Not only that...
On April 2, 2023, in Lucknow, India.
Prem Rawat set the Guinness world record for the largest audience ever to attend a book reading by a single author. The official audience tally for his reading of Hear Yourself was 114,704 people, which far exceeds the previous record of 6,786.
