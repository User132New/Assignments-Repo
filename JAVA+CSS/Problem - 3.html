Once Upon a Time...

In a world full of technology, a mysterious ufo appeared. People gathered, drawn by its strange colors. No one could understand whether it was a portal to another world or a tear in space reality?

The team of Scientists was blown-away and could not decide on what to do next. After a while, order were issued by government and military sent a craft with Kavya inside. She encountered strange phenomenons. Time and physics behaved strangely. As she went ahead, the ufo vanished, trapping her inside!!!

After 10 Years, Back on Earth, the ufo was forgotten. As time passed, people on Earth moved on, forgetting the incident, as if it never happened. While Kavya adapted, learning to survive.

Kavya never grew older, never became hungry or thirsy. She suvived for years, feeling the loneliness. She wondered if her sacrifice had mattered. She looked at a starless sky, forever lost in a world where time doesn't exist and just seemed like another pipe-dream.
